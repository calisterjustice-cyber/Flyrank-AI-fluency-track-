### Three Target Tasks (for FL-02 through FL-04)

**1. LinkedIn/X content drafting**
*Done well means:* Post reflects my authentic voice (no generic AI phrasing), makes one honest claim I can back up, and takes under 15 minutes from draft to publish-ready.

**2. Curriculum materials for New Wine Academy**
*Done well means:* Content is NERDC-aligned, age-appropriate, and could be handed to a teacher with zero further edits needed — checked against the standard, not just "sounds good."

**3. Client deliverables (e.g., payment reconciliation tracker, VA role-fit docs)**
*Done well means:* Formulas/data are 100% accurate on a test run, formatting matches professional standard, and it's something a client could use immediately without asking "wait, what does this mean?"
