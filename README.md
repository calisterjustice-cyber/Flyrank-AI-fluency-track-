Flyrank AI fluency internship deliverables 
## Week 1 — Proof Statement

**Proof statement:** I help SaaS founders turn confusing, frustrating live chat moments into calm, clear resolutions — by putting myself in the customer's shoes and explaining things simply, without letting them feel dismissed. A founder wearing every hat doesn't have time to write out a job description and vet ten applicants; they need to see, in one transcript, that I can be trusted with their customers. I want them to read my portfolio and email me directly to start a conversation.

**One-line why:** A CV says I've "handled customer inquiries" — a portfolio lets a founder actually watch me de-escalate a frustrated customer and make a confusing answer click, which is the one thing they can't take on faith.
